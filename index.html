A Note From The Artist

"This song is for anyone who's ever felt like a ghost in their own circle. It's about being the backup plan, the convenient friend. As my first project, I wrote, composed, and sang every line to capture that raw feeling of being unseen. This is my story, and I'm sharing it in the hopes it connects with yours."
